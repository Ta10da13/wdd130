Purpose

The purpose of this web site to guide and help individuals, schools, companies or organisations to get their work done easy getting staionery, furniture, sportsware and textbooks from a trusted company like Shalom Bookshop.

Audience

People will come to my site because thats where they will find most of their school, company, organisations and individual stationery textbooks and furniture. So it is a one stop site where they will find all they need for their office and individual work to be done. We expect our site to answer so many questions for our valued visitors and customers such as the type of products that they would like to be supplied. Actions that needs to be completed on our site is to register with us then get access to most of our products and services.

Textbooks

We provide textbooks for Early childhood infants new curriculum, primary new curriculum textbooks, secondary new curriculum textbooks for both public and private schools and universities. This will be targeting all learning sectors that required textbooks and written information that are a must to their disciplines. There are actions that needs to be completed on our site is to register with us then get access to most of our products and services.

Furniture

We provide stationery for Early childhood infants, primary chairs, bench and table, secondary chairs, paded chairs, swivel chairs for both public and private schools and universities. This will be targeting all learning sectors as well as organisations that require furniture.
