Purpose

The purpose of this web site to guide and help online traders to a successful trading plan and a successful trading building a revenue on an existing business.

Audience

My audience is going be all of the online traders who they trade futures, currencies, metals, etc...These are kind of people who spent most their time analysing online markets as well as on the internet aging form 16 to 50 years of age most probably in countries that use stable currencies. This audience had been ignored for a long period of time without their needs being met which are trading schedules, trading plan and information on how to trade for newbies in the trading world. The audience will access my site using laptops, Cellphones, desktops and other smart gadgets that have internet access.
